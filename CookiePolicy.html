    COOKIE POLICY

    This Cookie Policy explains how LUXIMA.ID ("we," "us," or "our") uses cookies and similar technologies on our website (the "Site"). By using our Site, you consent to our use of cookies in accordance with this policy.

    What are cookies?

    Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet, etc.) when you visit a website. They are used to remember your preferences, track your activity, and personalize your experience.

    What types of cookies do we use?

      We use the following types of cookies on our Site:
    • Strictly necessary cookies: These cookies are essential for the Site to function properly. They allow you to navigate the Site and use its features.
    • Performance cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use the Site, such as which pages you visit most often. They help us improve the Site's performance and provide a better user experience.
    • Functionality cookies: These cookies remember your preferences and personalize your experience on the Site. For example, they can remember your username and language preferences.
    • Advertising cookies: These cookies are used to show you ads that are relevant to your interests. They can also be used to track your activity across different websites and devices.

    How do we use cookies?

      We use cookies for the following purposes:
    • To remember your preferences and personalize your experience on the Site.
    • To improve the Site's performance and provide a better user experience.
    • To show you ads that are relevant to your interests.
    • To track your activity across different websites and devices.

    How to manage cookies?

    You can manage cookies through your web browser settings. Most web browsers allow you to control cookies, including whether or not to accept them, and how to remove them.

    Please note that if you disable cookies, some features of the Site may not work properly, and your experience may be less personalized.

    Changes to this policy

    We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
